About Me

Hi there! My name is Joseph, and I’m a huge fan of physical media, music, and all kinds of cool nostalgic stuff. I started this website to share my passions, document my collections, and connect with like-minded people.

I got tired of the algorithm-driven, AI-heavy internet and wanted to create my own space where I have full control. On this site, you'll find updates about my collections, blogs about my interests, and maybe even some flash games or music projects.

Feel free to reach out at josephshipley2004@gmail.com. I reply as soon as I can, but not instantly—so keep that in mind!
